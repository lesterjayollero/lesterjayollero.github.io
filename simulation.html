Multi-Lane Traffic Simulation

This interactive demo visualizes a simplified two-lane microsimulation model. Each vehicle follows its leader but can change lanes when encountering slower traffic or smaller headways. This model demonstrates lane-changing behavior and flow dynamics commonly analyzed in traffic engineering research.
