Interactive Traffic Simulation

This simulation illustrates basic traffic flow dynamics using a simplified microsimulation model. Each vehicle follows a leader–follower behavior: it accelerates when the front gap increases and decelerates when congestion builds up. This interactive demo serves as a visualization tool for research involving traffic operations, vehicle headways, and lane utilization.
