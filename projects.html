Project Involvements

  • Philippine Active Transport Strategic Master Plan (ATSMP) Led the training of field enumerators for the nationwide household interview survey on active transport mode choice across all NCR cities and pilot sites : Puerto Princesa, Iloilo, Zamboanga, Mati, and Surigao.
    Conducted participatory infrastructure audits.
  • Local Public Transport Route Plan (LPTRP) Ongoing
  • Research Health Institute Transportation Master Plan Ongoing
  • Taguig Traffic Study Led field traffic surveys and prepared traffic analysis reports, evaluating traffic flow characteristics, congestion patterns, and operational performance of key road sections.
  • Smart City Traffic Study Evaluated the development's traffic generation and its impacts on both the internal and external road networks.
  • Clark Global City Transportation Master Plan Led field surveys and analyzed road connectivity to support master planning of Clark Global City. Conducted gateway and link road capacity analysis and forecasted long-term traffic conditions under various development scenarios.
